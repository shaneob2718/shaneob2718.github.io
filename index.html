About Me

I'm Shane O'Brien, a recent physics graduate from the University of California, Irvine. My main area of research interest is experimental particle physics, particularly data analysis in new particle searches, accelerator improvements/upgrades, and using machine learning to better detect particles in collider data. In addition to these interests, I also have previous experience working in observational exoplanet astronomy research, as well as in experimental astro-particle physics research.

When I am not doing physics, I enjoy going for long walks, practicing my Chess skills, reading, playing video games, and working on personal programming projects.
